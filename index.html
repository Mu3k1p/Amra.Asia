About Amra

More than
just coffee.

Amra was built with intention. The design pulls from New Khmer Architecture of the 1960s — clean geometry, open airflow, and materials that belong to this place. It doesn't feel like a trendy import. It feels rooted.

The ground floor lets the river breeze move through. Upstairs is cool and quiet. Power outlets throughout. Khmer songs from the 60s on the speakers, mixed with a little modern pop at a volume that lets you actually talk.

One reviewer put it well: it's a labor of love, not just another coffee shop.
